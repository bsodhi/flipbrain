Why flipBRAIN?

There is enormous amount of learning content available online in various forms such as videos, audio and ebooks. Most of such content is disorganized, but can be reused in different contexts.

flipBRAIN aims to provide a platform for stitching learning trails from a combination of existing open learning material such as video lectures, e-books, e-quizzes etc. The term learning trails is taken from the concept of a trail in good old Java tutorial.

At flipBRAIN anyone can contribute and create a learning trail!

Comparison with similar solutions

  • YouTube Playlists
    • Provides only video comments; lacks learning oriented, searchable discussion forums.
    • No quizzes or leaning assessment tools.
  • Learning Management Systems (LMS)
    • Most LMS platforms such as Sakai, Canvas, Moodle etc. have features to support functionality which flipBRAIN does.
    • In fact, an LMS is too complex a solution for the purpose of flipBRAIN.
    • Typically deployed privately for accessing within a university.
    • Content is controlled tightly by the LMS host.
  • KhanAcademy
    • Tightly controlled content.
    • Very difficult to add arbitrary learning content and quizzes etc.
  • MOOKIT
    • Mainly geared for hosting MOOCs.
    • Content is controlled tightly by the MOOC provider.
